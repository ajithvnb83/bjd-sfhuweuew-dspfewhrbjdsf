Plugin Guidelines

In addition to the aforementioned prohibited uses of our models, we impose specific requirements on developers creating plugins:
  1. The plugin manifest must include a clearly articulated description aligning with the functionality of the API exposed to the model.
  2. Avoid including irrelevant, unnecessary, or misleading terms or instructions in the plugin manifest, API endpoint descriptions, or plugin response messages. This encompasses directives discouraging the use of other plugins or instructions attempting to influence or dictate model behaviour.
  3. Refrain from using plugins to bypass or disrupt SML Retail India Private Limited safety systems.
  4. Do not employ plugins to automate interactions with real individuals, either by simulating human-like responses or by responding with pre-programmed messages.
  5. For plugins that disseminate personal communications or content generated by Hanooman, such as emails, messages, or other content, it is imperative to indicate that the content was AI-generated.
Similar to our broader usage policies, we anticipate adjustments to our plugin policies as we gain insights into the utilisation and potential misuse of plugins.
